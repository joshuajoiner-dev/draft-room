# Founding Day

**July 4, 2026**

Today JoinDraftPick became publicly available.

It was a quiet opening, not a loud launch. The doors opened after the first verified commerce pipeline was complete: Shopify purchase, `orders/paid` webhook, Supabase license, Postmark unlock email, and browser unlock.

The goal is simple: help people spend less time organizing and more time playing, teaching, coaching, and leading.

JoinDraftPick is for teachers, coaches, recreation leaders, organizers, and anyone who has ever lost time picking teams.

The Lite Version is always free.

JoinDraftPick Complete became available after the commerce path was proven end to end. It exists for organizers who need the fuller toolkit, while the core promise remains clear:

**One Organizer.**

**One Device.**

**One Minute.**

This is not the end of development. It is the opening of the doors.

From here, the work continues: better rooms, faster setup, stronger team tools, clearer organizer flows, and a product that stays focused on the moment people actually came for.

Play should start sooner.
